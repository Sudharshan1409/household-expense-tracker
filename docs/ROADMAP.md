# Roadmap

## Stage 1 — Reliable core
Authentication, household, invitations, categories, payment methods, expense CRUD, correct splits.

Exit criterion: two separate accounts can join one household, both add expenses, and both see mathematically correct shared data.

## Stage 2 — Useful daily dashboard
Month/member filtering, KPI cards, trends, category/payment charts, recent/top expenses.

Exit criterion: dashboard answers the primary monthly questions without manual calculations.

## Stage 3 — Planning
Budgets, income, savings, calendar and reports.

## Stage 4 — Shared-finance intelligence
Settlements and recurring expenses.

## Stage 5 — Mobile quality
PWA, offline behavior, sync/retry.

## Stage 6 — Portability
CSV/XLSX/PDF/JSON exports.

## Stage 7 — Hardening
Security, accessibility, performance, observability, deployment.

## Future
OCR, voice entry, merchant prediction, vehicle/trip modules, credit-card statements, net worth.
